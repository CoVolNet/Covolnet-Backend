  • Why CoVolNet?

    Because we believe that the current situation in India is out of control and there are thousands of people who need help.

  • Who runs CoVolNet?

    CoVolNet, short form for Covid Volunteers Network, is an unofficial non-profit network of individuals willing to put time and effort to curate a repository of list of essential services available.

  • How does CoVolNet work?

    Volunteering individuals verify each and every information coming to us. When we receive a call for help, we try our level best to find a service provider that can help the situation. We update our database continuously in order to make sure that there are no dysfunctional entries in our records.

  • Which all areas do CoVolNet provide service in?

    We strive hard to provide our services all over India. We are a volunteer-run, crowdsourced initiative who needs like-minded individuals aiming to provide our services all over India. We need massive volunteer support for this initiative, and we urge you to join so that CoVolNet is available in your area as well. If you are ready, do join as a volunteer!
